welcome to the department of computer science

The Sitamarhi Institute of Technology (SIT) stands as a testament to the exceptionalquality of government-funded institutions in India. Nestled in the picturesque district ofSitamarhi in Bihar, SIT was founded in 2016 and has quickly garnered a reputation as apremier engineering college. It offers a comprehensive 4-year B.Tech program across sixmajor engineering disciplines: Civil Engineering, Mechanical Engineering, ElectricalEngineering, Computer Science & Engineering, Artificial Intelligence and MachineLearning, and Civil Engineering - Computer Applications. Affiliated with Bihar EngineeringUniversity, Patna, and sanctioned by the esteemed All India Council for TechnicalEducation, New Delhi, SIT continues to uphold its commitment to academic excellence
